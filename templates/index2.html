Analysis by a digital map (Orthomosaic), constructed

Statistical application

* Analysis simulation


Photo by photo analysis, where each photo represents a percentage of the production area.

The count values ​​are extrapolated to obtain an estimate of the total area, which makes possible the application in areas of great extensions, where to build a high resolution orthomosaics is unfeasible.
